## Description (App Store style — narrative flow, no bullet points at opening)

AutoCareX is India's most comprehensive car care and automobile service platform, designed for every car owner who values their time, safety, and money.

Whether your car needs a quick wash before an important meeting, an oil change that's long overdue, an emergency tyre replacement on the highway, or a full engine service at home — AutoCareX connects you with verified, professional service partners in your city within minutes. No more unplanned trips to crowded service centres. No more surprise bills. Just reliable car care, on your schedule.

**Book Any Car Service in Under 30 Seconds**

The AutoCareX home screen puts every service category at your fingertips — car wash and detailing, periodic engine service, tyre change and balancing, battery replacement, AC servicing, dent and paint, oil change, roadside assistance, and more. Pick your service, choose a convenient date and time slot, confirm your location, and you're done. A verified service partner accepts your booking and heads your way.

**Live GPS Tracking — Know Exactly When They Arrive**

Once a partner accepts your booking, a live map view shows their real-time location as they travel to you. The app displays the estimated arrival time and updates it as conditions change. After service is complete, you receive a detailed digital report with timestamps, parts used, and before-and-after photos — all saved in your booking history.

**Roadside Assistance, Anytime You Need It**

Flat tyre at 2 AM? Dead battery in a parking lot? AutoCareX Roadside Assistance operates 24 hours a day, 7 days a week across 25+ Indian cities. One tap sends a trained responder with the right tools — jump-start cables, tow truck, tyre inflator, fuel can, or lockout kit. Response times average under 30 minutes in metro areas.

**Transparent Pricing, No Hidden Charges**

Every booking displays a fixed quote before you confirm — no surprise additions at the end. Genuine parts, OEM-grade consumables, and a 7-day service guarantee back every job. Pay securely via UPI, debit/credit card, net banking, or wallet, and receive a GST-compliant digital invoice instantly.

**AutoCareX Care+ Subscription**

Members enjoy priority booking slots, discounted rates across all services, free roadside assistance calls each month, and a dedicated relationship manager. Plans start at ₹199 per month — the most affordable way to ensure your car is always in top condition.

**Join the Partner Network**

Mechanics, detailers, tyre technicians, and automobile professionals across India use AutoCareX to build and grow their business. Access verified customer bookings, digital payment settlement, training resources, and real-time performance dashboards — all from the AutoCareX Partner app.

AutoCareX is trusted by 10,000+ car owners and 500+ service professionals. Download the app, add your vehicles, and experience car care reimagined for modern India.
